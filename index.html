About Us

What makes our dream team work - our secret ingredient - is our shared passion for food and family. You'll feel it when Mel or Andie warmly welcome you, when our wait team enthuse about the day's specials. When you taste the freshest local ingredients that our chefs lovingly prepare, or the oven-hot goodness conjured by our pizza team. Our passion is the culmination of our shared lifelong experience of food bringing friends and family together, and our team's combined century or two in the food, hospitality and people industries. It means you can expect food that's delicious and nourishing!
